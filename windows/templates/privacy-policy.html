SecureStart Privacy Policy

Welcome to SecureStart, the app so secure even I sometimes forget the password. Below is my super-serious, totally legally-binding privacy policy.

What I Collect

  • Absolutely nothing… except maybe your name, email, and the fact that most people really like continuing without even reading what they're agreeing to!
  • I may collect cookies, but only the digital kind. For chocolate chip, please see the kitchen.

How I Use Your Data

  • To make sure you can log in without summoning the IT gods.
  • To keep hackers out (they never read my privacy policy anyway).
  • To occasionally laugh at the number of failed login attempts you made before remembering your password.

Who I Share Your Data With

  • Nobody. Not even my mom.
  • Unless someone offers me a lifetime supply of tacos, then I might reconsider.

Your Responsibilities

  • Don’t use “password123” as your password. I will judge you.
  • If you forget your password, don’t panic, that’s what “Forgot Password” buttons are for.
  • Do not attempt to socially engineer me with free pizza. It might work.

Security

I lock your data in a box, inside another box, inside another box, inside a virtual dragon’s lair guarded by flaming CAPTCHA riddles. My keys are so random, even Vegas is impressed.

Changes to this Policy

I may update this policy whenever I get bored or find a funnier meme. If you don’t like the new policy, you can always close the tab.
